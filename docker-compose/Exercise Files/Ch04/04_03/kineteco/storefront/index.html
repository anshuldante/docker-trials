Consumer Products and Gadgets

K-Eco Solar Panel

Available in 200, 250, and 300-watt models. Designed for use in a grid and/or battery backup system. Our solar panels are rated the highest in the industry for energy savings and economic value. Our panels, when combined with K-Eco inverters and batteries, power the most efficient photovoltaic systems for residential and business uses.

K-Eco Energy Bulb

Our CFL bulbs shine with a pure, white light and use 75% less energy than incandescent bulbs, while providing the same amount of light. They’re available in a range of outputs from 9 to 70 watts.

K-Eco Phone Charger

Our portable phone charger provides up to 18 hours of power, even on a cloudy day. It also features our rapid solar charge technology, which allows the unit to become fully charged after only two hours in full to partial sun

K-Eco Mini Panel

Our 6-Watt 12-Volt Mini Panel is a mere 4-inch x 3-inch.  This handheld solar device takes only 6 to 10 hours to charge in partial to full sun, and provides twice that time in powering your favorite electronics including portable music players, phones, game consoles, and more.
